Why I Started This Blog

May 23, 2025 1 min read Selfmusing by Jiv

Every day, I come across ideas that prompt me to think: fleeting thoughts, behavioral patterns, global trends, or everyday moments. However, those ideas often go unspoken, unexplored or unshared. I created Selfmusing by Jiv to change this. This blog is a space for mindful thought, a place where I can explore my interdisciplinary interests: international relations, psychology, data, and culture through writing and reflection. Instead of waiting for formal opportunities to explore these ideas, I created my own forum to engage with them freely and consistently. Just as significantly, I built this space to encourage dialogue. I believe that some of the best insights begin as quiet musings and blossom through mutual reflection. I hope to meet others who are just as curious. If something here resonates with you, I'd love to hear your thoughts. This is an open invitation to read, respond, and discover, together. You can reach out to me via email at jivpandit@gmail.com or connect with me on LinkedIn. Whether it’s feedback, a shared idea or a new opportunity, I’m always open to thoughtful dialogue and reflective exchange.

With curiosity, Jiv
