Data Science Decoded: How It Shapes Your Day and Why I'm Hooked

May 25, 2025 2 min read Selfmusing by Jiv

As I begin my journey into applied data analytics, I’ve been reflecting on the significance of data science and its impact on our daily lives.

At its core, data science transforms raw data into valuable insights. It’s where coding meets statistics and domain knowledge, revealing hidden patterns and answers to important questions. It’s more than just crunching numbers; it’s about discovering the story within the data.

What makes data science even more intriguing is how it shapes our everyday experiences. It powers apps and tools we often take for granted, like Netflix recommendations, ride-sharing apps and email filters. These algorithms make our digital lives smoother and more personalized, often working behind the scenes without us even noticing.

I decided to pursue a Minor in Applied Data Analytics because I’m drawn to the hands-on, problem-solving aspect of this field. Applied data analytics brings data science principles to life, tackling real-world challenges in areas like healthcare, business, and technology.

I know I’m only scratching the surface, but I’m genuinely excited about what lies ahead. There’s so much to learn and I can’t wait to dive deeper into how data can drive meaningful change and innovation.

If you’re curious about how data science shapes the world, or if you’re just getting started, I’d love to hear your thoughts. This is only the beginning for me, so stay tuned for more!
